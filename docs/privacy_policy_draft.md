# Sherbrt Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 22, 2026**

Welcome to Sherbrt! 💖

This Privacy Policy explains how Shop on Sherbrt, Inc. ("Sherbrt," "we," "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our website, mobile app, and services (collectively, the "Service").

By using Sherbrt, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide Directly

- **Account information:** name, email address, password, phone number, profile photo, and profile preferences (including height, size preferences, and similar profile details shared voluntarily).
- **Listing content:** item descriptions, photographs, condition notes, brand, size, color, retail value, and other attributes you enter when listing items.
- **Transaction information:** shipping addresses, billing addresses, booking dates, and messages exchanged through the platform.
- **Identity verification information:** where required, we may collect government-issued identification (such as a driver's license or passport) to verify your identity, prevent fraud, or comply with legal obligations.
- **Support communications:** messages, questions, and feedback you send to us at bestie@sherbrt.com or through the platform.

### Information We Collect Automatically

- **Device and technical data:** IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers, mobile carrier, and crash logs.
- **Usage data:** pages viewed, features used, buttons tapped, search queries, time spent on screens, and referral source.
- **Cookies and similar technologies:** see Section 4 (Cookies & Tracking Technologies) below.
- **Location information:** approximate location derived from IP address, and (with your permission) precise location from your mobile device for features like local-listing discovery or shipping estimates.

### Information We Receive from Third Parties

- **Payment processors:** Stripe provides us with transaction outcomes (success/failure, payment method type), cardholder name, and partial card details (last 4 digits, expiration, brand). **We never receive or store your full payment card number.**
- **Shipping partners:** Shippo and carriers provide tracking events, delivery confirmations, and address verification data.
- **Analytics and authentication partners:** aggregated behavior data from analytics providers, and sign-in confirmation from OAuth providers if you log in via a connected account.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Create, operate, and maintain your Sherbrt account and listings
- Process rentals, payments, refunds, late fees, replacement charges, and damage fees
- Send booking confirmations, shipping updates, return reminders, and other transactional communications
- Send support replies and account-related messages
- Detect, prevent, and investigate fraud, misrepresentation, or abuse of the platform
- Verify identity and comply with applicable legal obligations (including tax reporting where required)
- Improve the Service by analyzing usage patterns and user feedback
- Personalize your experience (showing relevant listings, remembering preferences)
- Enforce our Terms of Service and protect the rights and safety of Sherbrt and our users

**We only use your information for the purposes described in this policy.**

### SMS Notifications

If you provide your phone number and opt in, we may send you SMS messages about your account, including booking confirmations, shipping reminders, return reminders, late fee notices, and support updates. Message and data rates may apply. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message.

## 3. Who We Share Your Information With

**We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.**

We share personal information only as needed to operate and improve Sherbrt, specifically with:

- **Payment processors** (e.g., Stripe) — to process rental payments, refunds, and authorized charges.
- **Shipping partners** (e.g., Shippo, USPS, UPS, FedEx) — to create shipping labels, track shipments, and facilitate returns.
- **Communication providers** (e.g., Twilio for SMS, SendGrid for email) — to deliver transactional notifications.
- **Analytics providers** — to measure platform usage and improve features. Providers we use may include Google Analytics and similar services.
- **Cloud hosting and infrastructure providers** — to securely host the Service and associated data.
- **Professional advisors** (attorneys, accountants, auditors) — as needed for legal compliance and business operations.
- **Legal authorities** — when required by law, subpoena, court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Sherbrt, our users, or others.
- **Business successors** — in the event of a merger, acquisition, reorganization, or sale of assets, subject to confidentiality protections.

## 4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Sherbrt uses cookies and similar technologies to:

- Keep you logged in across sessions
- Remember your preferences
- Measure platform performance and understand how features are used
- Detect and prevent fraud

You can manage or delete cookies in your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect how the Service functions (e.g., requiring you to log in every visit).

### Do Not Track

Some browsers send a "Do Not Track" (DNT) signal. Because there is no industry consensus on how DNT signals should be interpreted, Sherbrt does not currently respond to DNT signals. We will update this policy if that changes.

## 5. Your Privacy Choices

Regardless of where you live, you can:

- **Update your account information** at any time through your profile settings.
- **Delete your account** by emailing bestie@sherbrt.com. We will delete your personal information subject to the retention exceptions described below.
- **Unsubscribe from non-essential emails** via the unsubscribe link in any marketing email.
- **Opt out of SMS notifications** by replying STOP to any SMS.
- **Request a copy of your personal information** by emailing bestie@sherbrt.com.

## 6. California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA).

### Categories of Personal Information We Collect

In the past 12 months, we may have collected the following categories of personal information (as defined by CCPA):

- **Identifiers** — name, email address, phone number, account username, IP address, device identifiers.
- **Personal information under Cal. Civ. Code § 1798.80(e)** — name, address, phone number, payment information (as transmitted to Stripe).
- **Protected classification characteristics** — age (via 18+ attestation); we do not collect race, religion, gender identity, sexual orientation, medical information, or similar protected classes unless voluntarily provided in profile fields.
- **Commercial information** — rental history, items listed, items borrowed, booking details, payment amounts.
- **Internet or other electronic network activity** — pages viewed, features used, referral sources, time on platform.
- **Geolocation data** — approximate location from IP; precise location from mobile device only with your permission.
- **Sensory data** — photographs uploaded to listings and profiles.
- **Inferences** — aggregated preferences derived from your platform usage (e.g., style preferences, size preferences).
- **Sensitive personal information** — government-issued ID when required for identity verification; account log-in credentials. We do NOT use sensitive personal information for purposes other than those permitted by CPRA (identity verification, security, legal compliance, and service delivery).

### Sources of Personal Information

We collect personal information from: you directly, your device automatically as you use the Service, payment processors, shipping partners, analytics providers, and authentication providers (if you use connected accounts).

### Business Purposes for Use

We use personal information for the purposes listed in Section 2 above, all of which fall within CCPA-recognized business purposes (auditing, security, fraud prevention, service delivery, quality assurance, R&D, and legal compliance).

### Sale and Sharing of Personal Information

**Sherbrt does not sell personal information.** Sherbrt does not share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising. We have not sold or shared personal information for these purposes in the past 12 months and have no plans to do so.

### Your California Rights

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

- **Know** what personal information we have collected about you, the sources, the purposes, and the categories of third parties we share it with.
- **Delete** personal information we hold about you, subject to exceptions (for example, information needed to complete a transaction, detect fraud, comply with legal obligations, or exercise free speech).
- **Correct** inaccurate personal information we hold about you.
- **Opt out** of the sale or sharing of personal information (not applicable to Sherbrt since we don't sell or share for advertising, but you have the right regardless).
- **Limit the use of sensitive personal information** to purposes permitted by CPRA.
- **Not be discriminated against** for exercising any of these rights. We will not deny you the Service, charge different prices, or provide a different level of quality based on your exercise of privacy rights.
- **Appeal** any denial of a rights request.

### How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any California privacy right, email **bestie@sherbrt.com** with the subject "California Privacy Request" and include enough information to verify your identity (such as the email address and phone number on your account). We may request additional information to verify your identity before fulfilling a request involving sensitive data.

**Response timeline:** We will confirm receipt of your request within 10 business days and respond substantively within 45 calendar days. If we need more time (up to an additional 45 days), we will notify you of the extension and the reason.

**Authorized agents:** You may designate an authorized agent to submit requests on your behalf. We will require the agent to provide written proof of authorization and may contact you to verify the request.

**Appeals:** If we deny your request, you may appeal by replying to our denial email within 30 days. We will respond to your appeal within 45 days.

## 7. Other U.S. State Privacy Rights

If you are a resident of a U.S. state with a comprehensive privacy law, you may have additional privacy rights. States with such laws include (but are not limited to) Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, Utah, Oregon, Texas, Montana, Iowa, Delaware, Tennessee, Minnesota, Maryland, New Hampshire, New Jersey, Kentucky, Rhode Island, and others, with new state laws continuing to take effect. While specifics vary by state, these laws typically provide the right to:

- **Access** and obtain a copy of your personal information
- **Correct** inaccurate personal information
- **Delete** your personal information
- **Opt out** of the sale of personal information or its use for targeted advertising (not applicable to Sherbrt since we do not engage in either, but you retain the right regardless)
- **Not be discriminated against** for exercising these rights

**To exercise your rights under your state's law,** email **bestie@sherbrt.com** with the subject "Privacy Request" and include your state of residence and enough information for us to verify your identity. We will respond within the timeframe required by your state's law (typically 30–45 days).

**Universal opt-out signals (Global Privacy Control).** Where required by applicable state law, Sherbrt honors the Global Privacy Control (GPC) signal. If your browser sends a valid GPC signal, we treat it as a request to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information for that browser session.

**Appeals.** If we deny your request, you may appeal by replying to our denial email. We will respond within the timeframe required by your state's law.

## 10. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy:

- **Account information:** for the lifetime of your account, plus up to 12 months after account closure for fraud prevention and dispute resolution.
- **Transaction and rental records:** up to 7 years after the transaction, as required by tax and financial record-keeping laws.
- **Support communications:** up to 3 years after resolution.
- **Analytics data:** aggregated and de-identified after 14 months.
- **Legal hold:** longer, if required by law, subpoena, or ongoing dispute.

When the retention period expires, we delete or de-identify the information.

We take your privacy seriously. We use industry-standard security practices to protect your data, including:

- Secure hosting with reputable cloud providers

That said, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. If we become aware of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you as required by applicable law.
