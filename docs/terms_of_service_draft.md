## Damage Assessment

Sherbrt will determine whether returned items are "damaged beyond reasonable repair" based on the following process:

1. **Report.** The lender reports damage to Sherbrt at bestie@sherbrt.com within 72 hours of receiving the returned item, including clear photographs and a description of the damage.
2. **Review.** Sherbrt reviews the submitted evidence. For ambiguous cases, Sherbrt may request evaluation from a qualified professional cleaner, tailor, or leather specialist.
3. **Response.** The borrower may submit counter-evidence or request a second opinion within 7 days of being notified of a damage determination.
4. **Determination.** Sherbrt's determination, in its reasonable discretion, will be based on whether the item can be restored to its pre-rental condition through standard cleaning or repair. Minor wear, routine cleaning needs, and expected rental use do not constitute "damaged beyond repair."
5. **Notice.** Damage charges will be itemized and communicated to the borrower in writing before being processed.
